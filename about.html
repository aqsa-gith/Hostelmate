The Best Way to predict your future is to create it - 104 years of excellence

The University of Engineering & Technology (UET) Lahore, established in 1921 as Mughalpura Technical College, is Pakistan's oldest and leading public engineering university, offering Bachelors, Masters, and PhDs in STEM fields.

  • History: Started as Mughalpura Technical College, became Maclagan Engineering College in 1923, and gained university status as UET in 1972.
  • Academics: Offers programs across Civil, Electrical, Mechanical, Computer Engineering, Architecture, and Business, with international recognition for OBE system.
  • Reputation: Top-tier, highly selective, known for academic excellence and research.
  • Campuses: Main Lahore campus and satellite campuses in Faisalabad, Kala Shah Kaku, and Narowal.
  • Impact: Graduates serving globally, fostering innovation through research and industry links.
