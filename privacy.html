Privacy Policy - HostelMate

Welcome to HostelMate! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information while using our web portal.

1. Information We Collect

When you register on HostelMate, we may collect:

  • Your name and contact information (email, phone number).
  • Login credentials for accessing the portal.
  • Images you upload (student photo, ID card) for registration purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To register you as a hostel member and manage your account.
  • To send registration confirmation codes via email or phone.
  • To display your profile and documents within the portal.

3. Sharing Information

We do not share your personal information with any third parties. All your data stays within HostelMate.

4. Cookies and Tracking

HostelMate does not use cookies or tracking for this demo project.

5. Your Rights

You can request to delete your data by contacting the developer. Since this is a demo project, no data is permanently stored.
